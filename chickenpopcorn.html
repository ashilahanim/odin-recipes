Chicken Popcorn

Popcorn Chicken consists of bite-sized pieces of crispy, deep-fried chicken, seasoned with spices for a flavorful crunch. It is a popular snack or fast-food item, often served with dipping sauces like honey mustard or spicy mayo.

    Ingredients:
  • 300g boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • Oil for frying
    Steps:
  1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, whisk together egg and milk.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In another bowl, combine flour, cornstarch, and seasonings.
  3. Coat the chicken: Dip chicken pieces in the egg mixture, then dredge in the flour mixture, pressing slightly to coat well.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 175°C.
  5. Fry the chicken in batches until golden brown and crispy (about 4-5 minutes).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve with dipping sauce.
