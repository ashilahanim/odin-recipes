Spaghetti

This scrumptious and delicious meal served with baked tomato puree sauce gives a tangy sour and sour taste of the tomato. Seasoned with parsely, thyme, rosemary and other herbs making it flavorful and aromaful.

    Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt and pepper (to taste)
  • ½ tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 200g ground beef (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)
    Steps:
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat, then sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the meat (if using) and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Toss the spaghetti in the sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top
