Mac 'N' Cheese

Mac and Cheese (Macaroni and Cheese) is a creamy and cheesy pasta dish made with elbow macaroni and a rich cheese sauce, typically using cheddar. It is a popular comfort food enjoyed baked or stovetop, often with breadcrumbs or extra cheese on top.

    Ingredients:
  • 200g elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp paprika (optional)
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs (optional, for baked version)
    Steps:
  1. Cook macaroni according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat, then whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Slowly pour in milk, whisking continuously until smooth.
  4. Add shredded cheese, stirring until melted and smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  5. Mix the pasta into the cheese sauce and stir to coat evenly.
  6. (Optional) If baking, transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and bake at 180°C for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
